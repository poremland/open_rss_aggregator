# Product Guidelines: Open RSS Aggregator

## Voice and Tone
- Professional, technical, and concise.
- Focus on API reliability and standards compliance (RSS, Atom, OPML).

## Design Principles
- Standards first: Adhere strictly to RSS, Atom, and OPML specifications.
- Security: Protect user data and authentication tokens.
- Performance: Offload heavy tasks (like feed fetching and imports) to background jobs.
